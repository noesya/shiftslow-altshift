You are invited to co-create the space, collectively caring for and about it for the time of the festival. 

The teams

Join the earth team to keep our place clean and beautiful.

Join our water team to keep the precious resource in its cycle and ourselves hydrated.

Join our kitchen team to provide us with wholesome vegan food.

The program

Friday
Arrival

Saturday
Landing process: discovering the land and people

Sunday to Thursday
A trip through four thematic streams

Radical Nature Connection
(Un)Building DeGrowth
Embodied Decoloniality
Prosocial Commons

In the mornings, we prepared you four amazing workshops corresponding to each of the thematic streams. Splitted in four groups, you will get to experience each of them throughout the week.

The afternoons will be dedicated to semi-spontaneous co-creation: do you want to organise a workshop? A performance? A sharing circle? A debating space? What do you want to bring to the festival? The floor is yours!

If you have an idea, get in touch with us and we'll provide you with the space and tools to realise it.

Friday
Rest, reflection & celebration: a local artisanal market and a groovy ending party will ensure the perfect ending to this amazing week!

Saturday
Packing up: leaving filled with connections, new tools for critical deconstruction, seeds of inspiration and zest for action!
