The programme below will keep evolving as more activities, workshops and participants' ideas are integrated (please contact us if you want to organise something).

What will not change, however, is the main goal of the festival: to provide an amazing space for the degrowth community to gather, organise and celebrate!

notes: >- - template: timeline title: >- The program position: 2 data: events: - title: >- Wednesday text: >- Arrival and landing process: discovering the land and people - title: >- Thursday to Saturday text: >- A trip through the three thematic streams (check below for more info) and many spaces for co-creation, sharing and experimentation. On Saturday, a groovy ending party will ensure the perfect closing to this amazing week! - title: >- Sunday text: >- Packing up: leaving filled with connections, new tools for critical deconstruction, seeds of inspiration and zest for action! - template: chapter title: >- position: 3 data: text: >-

On Thursday afternoon, Friday and Saturday morning, we prepared you three amazing workshops corresponding to each of the thematic streams. Split in groups, you will get to experience each one of them.

Friday and Saturday afternoon will be dedicated to semi-spontaneous co-creation: do you want to organise a workshop? A performance? A sharing circle? A debating space? What do you want to bring to the festival? The floor is yours!

If you have an idea, get in touch with us and we'll provide you with the space and tools to realise it.
